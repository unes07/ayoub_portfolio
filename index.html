Ayoub ABOULHAIBA

Aboulhaiba Ayoub, known as Aubeatz, is a Moroccan musician, composer and producer. His name appears in dozens of young rappers and singers musical projects, including the creation of the Glitch production, where he is member, producer and composer. His skillset includes songwriting, arranging, producing, conducting; piano/ keys, guitars, vocals; recording, editing, mixing, mastering; live performance and live sound. His debut was with Fasli Achraf in a mashup released in February 2018 and it's available in YouTube.
'I’ve always loved music, and I’ve always loved sound. Just a few months old, I started collecting music instruments and trying to play. As a teenager, I started creating my early first productions. By the time I was eighteen, I realized I was dissatisfied with my mixes so far and wanted my productions to sound like the ones of my heroes. This was when I decided to go professional in music and audio and help other musicians achieving the best quality possible with their own recordings.'
